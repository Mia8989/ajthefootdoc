A surgeon, scientist, and educator in one advisor.

Few consultants sit where Dr. Johnson does: in the operating room and the wound clinic, in front of medical students and residents, and inside the research literature. He is a board-certified podiatric surgeon and Certified Wound Specialist Physician, the first podiatrist to complete the University of Pennsylvania's Post-Doctoral Certificate in Clinical Research, and the author of 37 peer-reviewed publications.

That combination is what teams hire him for. He can pressure-test a product against real clinical workflow, shape a study that will actually publish, translate complex science for the people who need to act on it, and represent your work credibly to the clinicians you are trying to reach.
